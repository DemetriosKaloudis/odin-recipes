Santa Maria Grilled Tri-Tip Beef

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 (2 1/2 pound) beef tri-tip roast
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 4 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Stir salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, rosemary, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place tri-tip in a glass baking dish and coat on all sides with spice mixture. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrgerate for 4 hours.
  3. Combine vinegar, vegetable oil, crushed garlic, and dijon mustard in a sealable container. Cover the container and shake until blended.
  4. Remove tri-tip from the refrigerator. Let sit uncovered at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat outdoor grill on high-heat; lightly oil the grates
  6. Place tri-tip on the preheated grill and brush with vinegar mixture. Cook for 4 minutes flip, and baste. Flip and baste every 4 minutes until beef starts to firm up and is reddish-pink and juicy in the center, 25-30 minutes total. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 130 degrees F. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
