Garlic-Parmesan Shrimp

Ingredients

  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dred parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F. Spray medium glass casserole dish with cooking spray.
  2. Combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, parsley, and garlic powder in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal and shake to mix ingredients. Add shrimp and shake to coat evenly.
  3. Lay shrimp flat in the prepared casserole dish. Sprinkle and remaining mixture from bag. Drizzle melted butter over the top.
  4. Bake in preheated over until starting to crisp, 15-20 minutes should do. Switch to broil until topping is light brown, 2-3 minutes.
