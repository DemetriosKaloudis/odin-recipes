Baklava

Ingredients

  • Phyllo dough
  • Mix of walnuts and pistachios
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Simple syrup (water, sugar, vanilla, and honey)
  1. Layer phyllo dough with nuts. Stack 8 sheets of dough brushing with butter between each layer. Sprinkle with mixture of chopped nuts and cinnamon adding 2 layers of phyllo brushed with butter between nut layers. Continue this process until nut mixture is finished and top with 8 more layers of dough brushed with butter.
  2. Cut and bake. Cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes. Be sure to cut all the way to the pan. Bake until golden and crisp.
  3. Finish with syrup Boil water and sugar until sugar is melted. Add vanilla extract and honey. Remove the baklava from the oven and immediately pour syrup over it. Let cool before serving.
